Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please use our live chat feature for immediate assistance.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we provide support through email and live chat. Phone support is available for Enterprise plan subscribers only.

Can I schedule a demo or consultation?

Yes! We offer free 30-minute consultation calls for potential customers. Use the contact form above and select "Partnership Opportunity" to schedule a call.

Where can I report a bug or technical issue?

Please use the contact form and select "Technical Support" as the subject. Include as much detail as possible about the issue for faster resolution.
