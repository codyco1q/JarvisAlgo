Getting Started

Welcome to JarvisAlgo! This comprehensive guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start trading smarter with our AI-powered tools.

What You'll Need

  • An active JarvisAlgo subscription
  • A TradingView account (Free or Pro)
  • Internet connection
  • Your unique activation code (sent via email after registration)

Account Activation

After subscribing to JarvisAlgo, follow these steps to activate your account:

  1. Check your email for the welcome message containing your activation details
  2. Fill out the registration form with your TradingView username
  3. Our team will activate your access within 24 hours
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email with your indicator access link
Important: Make sure to use the same email address for both your JarvisAlgo subscription and TradingView account for seamless activation.

Installation & Setup

Step 1: Access Your Indicator

  1. Log in to your TradingView account
  2. Click on the activation link provided in your confirmation email
  3. The indicator will be added to your "Invite-only scripts" in TradingView

Step 2: Add to Your Chart

  1. Open any chart on TradingView
  2. Click on "Indicators" at the top of the chart
  3. Go to "Invite-only scripts" section
  4. Find "JarvisAlgo" and click to add it to your chart

Step 3: Configure Settings (Optional)

JarvisAlgo comes with optimized default settings, but you can customize them:

  1. Click the gear icon next to "JarvisAlgo" in your indicators list
  2. Adjust sensitivity, timeframes, and alert preferences
  3. Click "OK" to save your settings

Using JarvisAlgo Indicator

Understanding the Signals

JarvisAlgo provides clear, actionable trading signals directly on your charts:

BUY
Buy Signal (Green)

Indicates a potential long entry point. The AI has identified favorable conditions for an upward price movement.

SELL
Sell Signal (Red)

Indicates a potential short entry point or exit for long positions. The AI has detected conditions favoring downward price movement.

Reading the Dashboard

The JarvisAlgo dashboard displays key information at a glance:

  • Trend Direction: Shows whether the overall trend is bullish or bearish
  • Signal Strength: Indicates the confidence level of the current signal
  • Support/Resistance: Dynamic support and resistance levels
  • Risk Level: Current market volatility and risk assessment

Setting Up Alerts

  1. Right-click on the chart and select "Add Alert"
  2. Set the condition to "JarvisAlgo"
  3. Choose your notification method (Email, SMS, App notification)
  4. Configure alert frequency and expiration
  5. Click "Create" to activate the alert

Best Practices

  • Always use proper risk management and stop losses
  • Combine signals with your own analysis and market knowledge
  • Pay attention to signal strength indicators
  • Avoid trading during major news events without confirmation
  • Backtest strategies on multiple timeframes before live trading

Using Vision Screener

Accessing Vision Screener

  1. Log in to your JarvisAlgo client area
  2. Navigate to the "Vision Screener" section
  3. Your dashboard will display all available screening tools

Creating a Screen

Follow these steps to create a custom stock screen:

  1. Click "New Screen" or "Create Filter"
  2. Select your filter categories (Technical, Fundamental, or Both)
  3. Set your criteria for each filter (e.g., RSI > 70, Volume > 1M)
  4. Add multiple filters to narrow results
  5. Click "Run Screen" to see matching stocks

Popular Screen Examples

Momentum Breakout Screen

  • Price above 20-day MA
  • Volume > 150% of average
  • RSI between 50-70
  • 52-week high within 5%

Value Growth Screen

  • P/E Ratio < 20
  • Revenue growth > 20%
  • Market cap > $1B
  • Profit margin > 15%

Oversold Bounce Screen

  • RSI < 30
  • Price near support level
  • Positive earnings surprise
  • Institutional buying

Managing Watchlists

Keep track of promising stocks with custom watchlists:

  1. From your screen results, click the star icon next to any stock
  2. Create a new watchlist or add to an existing one
  3. Access your watchlists from the "My Watchlists" tab
  4. Set up alerts for watchlist stocks meeting specific criteria
  5. Export watchlists to CSV for further analysis

Setting Screener Alerts

  1. Save your custom screen
  2. Click "Set Alert" next to the saved screen
  3. Choose alert frequency (Real-time, Daily, Weekly)
  4. Select notification method
  5. Activate the alert

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overtrading: Don't force trades. Wait for high-quality setups.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are JarvisAlgo signals?

JarvisAlgo has been backtested across multiple markets and timeframes with strong performance metrics. However, no trading system is 100% accurate. Success depends on proper risk management and following best practices. Our AI continuously learns and adapts to market conditions to maintain high accuracy.

Can I use JarvisAlgo for day trading?

Absolutely! JarvisAlgo works on all timeframes from 1-minute charts to monthly charts. Many of our users successfully day trade using the 5-minute and 15-minute timeframes. The indicator provides real-time signals optimized for quick entries and exits.

Do I need TradingView Pro?

No, JarvisAlgo works with free TradingView accounts. However, a Pro account gives you access to more features like multiple chart layouts, more indicators per chart, and faster data updates, which can enhance your trading experience.

How often is the indicator updated?

Our development team continuously works on improvements and updates. Major updates are released quarterly, while minor bug fixes and optimizations happen as needed. All updates are automatic and free for active subscribers. You'll receive notifications in your client area when updates are available.

Can I share my subscription with others?

No, each subscription is licensed for individual use only and tied to your TradingView account. Sharing access violates our terms of service and may result in account termination. If you need multiple licenses for a team, please contact our sales department for volume pricing.

What if I need help or have technical issues?

Our support team is available 24/7 via live chat, email, and our support ticket system. We typically respond within 2 hours during business hours and within 24 hours on weekends. You can also access our comprehensive knowledge base and video tutorials in the client area.
